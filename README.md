# Answerproof

AI search visibility audits for B2B SaaS and high-value B2B services.

Answerproof answers one question:

> When buyers ask AI systems for vendors in your category, do they see you,
> your competitors, or nobody useful?

## What The Audit Includes

- Buyer-question map
- AI answer sampling across major answer engines
- Competitor mention and citation table
- Source-gap analysis
- 30-day fix plan

## Best Fit

- B2B SaaS teams with comparison-driven buying journeys
- Professional services firms where one qualified deal is valuable
- Teams already investing in SEO but not measuring AI-search visibility
- Companies with 2 to 5 known competitors buyers regularly compare
